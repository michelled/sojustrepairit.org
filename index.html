About

The goal of this project is to help youth movements and social justice initiatives to:

  • become welcoming environments for youth with learning differences, and
  • benefit from the advantages of inclusive design.

The project will provide a hub:

  • with toolkits, applications and examples
  • to share, repurpose and reuse resources.

The kit will be openly available to any group or individual hosting youth movements, youth action events and social justice movements.

Visit the wiki page for more information and project updates.

The IDRC has developed a Child Safeguarding Policy as part of this project to guide our work and the work of our partners, based on the guidelines and assistance of Keeping Children Safe.

Join Us!

This project is being created through iterative cycles of co-design with youth and supporting organisations.

If you are interested in being involved, please contact us.

We are particularly seeking stories from youth with learning differences, or from those who have worked or want to work with youth with learning differences, and challenges the project's work might address.
